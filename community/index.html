Join the Jenkins X community

Jenkins X is an open source project that anyone in the community can use, improve, and enjoy. We'd love you to join us! Here's a few ways to find out what's happening and get involved.

The Jenkins X Community is like no other, we are friendly, very open to feedback, provide guidance, best practices and much more. You can find us in various channels.

We value respect and inclusiveness and follow the CDF Code of Conduct in all interactions.

Please join us, we’d love to hear your feedback or have you participate and show some of the work you are doing with Jenkins X!

Joining the Community

Events

For details of the next Jenkins X demo or talk checkout our talks at conferences and meetups page!

Slack

We’d love to talk with you about Jenkins X and are happy to help if you have any questions.

Talk to us on our slack channels, which are part of the Kubernetes slack. Join Kubernetes slack here and find us on our channels:

  • #jenkins-x-user for users of Jenkins X
  • #jenkins-x-dev for developers of Jenkins X
